  • The Adventures of Kip (Unity): The Adventures of Kip
    This link goes to itch.io which is a free website that I unloaded my game to. Here is a brief description on the game:

    In my game design, I've taken a holistic approach, considering the emotional, surprising, and pleasurable aspects of gameplay. Through the lens of emotion, I aim to create an experience where players of all skill levels can find enjoyment and accomplishment, with careful attention to sound design enhancing immersion. I want players to feel a sense of achievement as they progress through the game, without it being too challenging. The lens of surprise is crucial to keeping players engaged and intrigued. I've incorporated elements like enemy sounds and unexpected boss levels to keep them on their toes. Additionally, I prioritize the pleasure of gameplay, aiming to provide a relaxing and enjoyable experience while still offering moments of excitement through combat. Overall, my goal is to create a well-rounded and captivating gaming experience that players can thoroughly enjoy.
